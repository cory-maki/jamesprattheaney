Meet Jim

30+ Years of Trusted Financial Guidance

Jim Pratt-Heaney co-founded Coastal Bridge Advisors with a clear mission: deliver institutional-caliber wealth management to individuals and families who deserve it. Over more than 30 years in financial services, Jim has built a reputation for rigorous analysis, sound judgment, and genuine relationships with the clients he serves.

Jim currently serves on the Advisory Board for Pershing Advisor Solutions and has been featured on Fox Business Channel and the Dow Jones Advisor Show. His commitment to education—through Marist College and continuing studies at Ohio State and The Wharton School of Management Center—reflects the same intellectual rigor he brings to every client engagement. You can read more on his full background and philosophy.

Outside the office, Jim is a family man, an avid guitarist, and an active presence in the Weston, CT community. He and his wife are surrounded by their children and growing families—a reminder of what long-term thinking is really for.
